A Few Beautiful Hiking Trails in San Francisco

Each hiking trail label contains an icon above it. Click on the icon to get more details and see a picture.

The icon on the top right of the map geolocates your current position so that you can see how far you are from the next trail.

On the top left corner of the map there are two input forms. One that allows you to add your own hiking trail to the map and the other to label them a name of your choosing. Have Fun!!!!
